# FDA Resources

FDA regulates animal drugs, medicated feeds, and food safety. These links help users find trusted veterinary and public health information.

## Top FDA Links

1. [FDA Home](https://www.fda.gov) — Main FDA site for human and animal health oversight.
2. [Animal & Veterinary](https://www.fda.gov/animal-veterinary) — Core animal health and veterinary oversight page.
3. [FDA on USA.gov](https://www.usa.gov/agencies/food-and-drug-administration) — Official agency summary and contact details.
4. [Animal Drugs @ FDA](https://www.accessdata.fda.gov/scripts/animaldrugsatfda/) — Search approved animal drugs and labels.
5. [FDA Veterinary Feed Directive](https://www.fda.gov/animal-veterinary) — Guidance on animal feed-related rules and use.
6. [FDA Animal Drug Labels](https://www.fda.gov/animal-veterinary) — Labeling and approved-use references.
7. [FDA CVM Safety Resources](https://www.fda.gov/animal-veterinary) — Animal safety and public health guidance.
8. [FDA Federal Register Agency Page](https://www.federalregister.gov/agencies/food-and-drug-administration) — Rulemaking and notices.
9. [FDA Veterinary Medicine Guidance](https://www.fda.gov/animal-veterinary) — Veterinary product and use guidance.
10. [FDA Animal & Veterinary Main Resources](https://www.fda.gov/animal-veterinary) — Primary veterinary resource hub.

## Best For

Use these resources when you need help with:
- Animal drugs.
