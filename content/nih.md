# NIH Resources

NIH supports biomedical research and public health science. These links are useful when a calculator or educational page needs research-based background.

## Top NIH Links

1. [NIH Home](https://www.nih.gov) — Main NIH site and research gateway.
2. [NIH on USA.gov](https://www.usa.gov/agencies/national-institutes-of-health) — Official NIH agency summary.
3. [National Cancer Institute](https://www.cancer.gov) — Cancer research and patient information.
4. [NIH News: Human-Based Research Technologies](https://www.nih.gov/news-events/news-releases/nih-prioritize-human-based-research-technologies) — NIH initiative and research direction.
5. [NCBI Books: Animal Care and Use Program](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK54045/) — Research animal care and use guidance.
6. [NIH News and Events](https://www.nih.gov/news-events) — Updates and announcements.
7. [NIH Research Training](https://www.nih.gov/research-training) — Training and grants information.
8. [NIH Grants and Funding](https://www.nih.gov/grants-funding) — Funding and grant mechanisms.
9. [NIH Almanac](https://www.nih.gov/about-nih/what-we-do/nih-almanac) — NIH structure and mission reference.
10. [NIH Main Institutes and Centers](https://www.nih.gov/institutes-nih) — Overview of NIH institutes.

## Best For

Use these resources when you need help with:
- Research background.
- Public health context.
- Biomedical science.
- Animal research references.
- Grant and training information.
