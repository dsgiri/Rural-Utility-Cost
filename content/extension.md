# Extension Resources

Extension services provide practical, research-based guidance for livestock, farming, and rural decision-making. These links are especially useful for cattle age, growth, ADG, and farm calculators.

## Top Extension Links

1. [Oklahoma State Beef Extension Calculators](https://extension.okstate.edu/programs/beef-extension/calculators) — Free factsheets, calculator instructions, and spreadsheets.
2. [Mississippi State: Estimating Cattle Age Using Dentition](https://extension.msstate.edu/publications/estimating-cattle-age-using-dentition) — Cattle age by teeth reference.
3. [Texas A&M Beef Extension Resources](https://animalscience.tamu.edu/extension-resources/) — Beef extension publications and tools.
4. [USU 4-H Beef Weight Calculator](https://extension.usu.edu/4H-Livestock-Calculator/beef-weight-calculator) — ADG and target-weight calculation.
5. [USU Livestock Calculators User Guide](https://extension.usu.edu/4H-Livestock-Calculator/user-guide) — Growth chart guidance and calculator use.
6. [Penn State Adjusting and Monitoring Meat Animal Growth Rate](https://extension.psu.edu/adjusting-and-monitoring-meat-animal-growth-rate/) — ADG and growth monitoring reference.
7. [Penn State Customized Dairy Heifer Growth Chart](https://extension.psu.edu/customized-dairy-heifer-growth-chart/) — Growth chart construction based on herd goals.
8. [University of Tennessee Age Determination in Beef Cattle](https://utbeef.tennessee.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/127/2020/11/AgeDeterminationinBeefCattle154.pdf) — Dentition reference for cattle age.
9. [FutureBeef: Ageing Cattle by Their Teeth](https://futurebeef.com.au/resources/ageing-cattle-by-their-teeth/) — Dentition table and breed variation.
10. [Montana State Average Daily Gain for the Fair](https://animalrangeextension.montana.edu/beef/articles/dailygain.html) — Simple ADG formula and example.

## Best For

Use these resources when you need help with:
- Cattle age estimation.
- Growth and ADG.
- Livestock management.
- Herd planning.
- Practical extension-based guidance.
