# USDA Resources

USDA provides essential programs and services for farmers, ranchers, and rural communities. These links help users find support for livestock, land, loans, conservation, rural development, and local service offices.

## Top USDA Links

1. [USDA Home](https://www.usda.gov) — Main USDA site covering agriculture, food, rural development, and farm programs.
2. [Farmers.gov](https://www.farmers.gov) — USDA hub for programs, tools, and support for farmers and ranchers.
3. [USDA Rural Development](https://www.rd.usda.gov) — Rural housing, water systems, business development, and community resources.
4. [Programs and Support for Small and Mid-Sized Farmers](https://www.usda.gov/farming-and-ranching/resources-small-and-mid-sized-farmers/programs-and-support-small-and-mid-sized-farmers) — Capital, land, conservation, and risk-management resources.
5. [Livestock Operation Resources](https://www.farmers.gov/your-business/livestock) — USDA livestock support, disaster assistance, and service center access.
6. [Resources for Small and Mid-sized Farmers](https://www.usda.gov/farming-and-ranching/resources-small-and-mid-sized-farmers) — General USDA support for producers and beginning farmers.
7. [Find Your Local USDA Service Center](https://www.farmers.gov/working-with-us/service-center-locator) — Locate FSA, NRCS, and Rural Development offices.
8. [USDA Agency Page](https://www.usa.gov/agencies/u-s-department-of-agriculture) — Official agency summary and contact gateway.
9. [Get Started at Your USDA Service Center](https://www.farmers.gov/working-with-us/USDA-service-centers) — How to prepare for a visit and what to bring.
10. [Farm Service Agency](https://www.fsa.usda.gov) — Loans, farm programs, and service access through USDA.

## Best For

Use these resources when you need help with:
- Farm and ranch programs.
- Livestock support.
- Rural development.
- Conservation.
- Disaster assistance.
- USDA service center access.
