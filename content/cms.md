# CMS Resources

CMS provides information about Medicare, Medicaid, CHIP, and other health coverage programs. These links are useful when a calculator or resource touches on healthcare coverage or provider enrollment.

## Top CMS Links

1. [CMS Home](https://www.cms.gov) — Main CMS website for Medicare, Medicaid, CHIP, and Marketplace oversight.
2. [CMS on USA.gov](https://www.usa.gov/agencies/centers-for-medicare-and-medicaid-services) — Official agency summary and contact details.
3. [Medicare.gov](https://www.medicare.gov) — Primary Medicare information and plan tools.
4. [Medicaid.gov](https://www.medicaid.gov) — Federal Medicaid policy and program information.
5. [CMS Innovation Center](https://www.cms.gov/priorities/innovation) — Innovation models and payment reform.
6. [CMS Provider Enrollment (PECOS)](https://pecos.cms.hhs.gov) — Enrollment system for Medicare providers.
7. [HealthCare.gov](https://www.healthcare.gov) — Health insurance marketplace information.
8. [CMS Quality, Safety & Value](https://www.cms.gov) — Quality improvement and program standards.
9. [State Medicaid Offices](https://www.usa.gov/agencies/centers-for-medicare-and-medicaid-services) — State contact pathways for Medicaid.
10. [CMS Agency Overview](https://www.usa.gov/agencies/centers-for-medicare-and-medicaid-services) — High-level official overview.

## Best For

Use these resources when you need help with:
- Medicare.
- Medicaid.
- CHIP.
- Provider enrollment.
- Health coverage research.
